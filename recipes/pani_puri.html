Pani Puri

Ingredients

  • 20 puris (small wheat puffs)
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves
  • 1 tablespoon jaggery
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 2 medium boiled potatoes
  • 1 small onion
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black salt
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water

Instructions

  1. Boil the potatoes, peel, and mash them. Finely chop the onion.
  2. In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes, boiled chickpeas, chopped onion, red chili powder, roasted cumin powder, chaat masala, black salt, and salt. Set aside as the filling.
  3. In a blender, combine coriander leaves, mint leaves, tamarind paste, jaggery, salt, and 4 cups of water. Blend until smooth to make the flavored pani (spiced water).
  4. Strain the pani through a fine sieve to remove any solids.
  5. Carefully make a small hole on the top of each puri.
  6. Fill each puri with a spoonful of the potato and chickpea filling.
  7. Dip the filled puri into the prepared pani or pour pani into the puri before eating.
  8. Serve immediately to enjoy the crispiness of the puris with the tangy, spicy water.

Enjoy your delicious Pani Puris!
