Samosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed for dough)
  • 3-4 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1/2 cup green peas (boiled)
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, salt, and oil. Mix well.
  2. Add water little by little and knead to form a firm, smooth dough. Cover and set aside for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat a little oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add chopped green chilies and grated ginger; saut√© for a minute.
  5. Add boiled potatoes, green peas, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Cook the mixture for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and add chopped coriander leaves. Let it cool.
  7. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each into a ball.
  8. Roll each ball into a thin oval or circle, then cut it in half to form two semi-circles.
  9. Take one semi-circle, fold it into a cone shape, and seal the edge with water.
  10. Fill the cone with the potato mixture, then seal the open edge carefully with water.
  11. Repeat with the remaining dough and filling.
  12. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  13. Deep fry the samosas until golden brown and crisp on all sides.
  14. Remove and drain on paper towels. Serve hot with chutney or sauce.

Enjoy your delicious Samosas!
